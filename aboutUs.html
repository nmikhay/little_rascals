About Us

WooHoo! is officially the LARGEST family amusement center in Canada! Believe it or not, it is right here in Montreal! It also has the LARGEST play structure in all of North America! What will you find at WooHoo?.


  • The awesome giant tornado slides
  • Immense labyrinth play structure
  • Ball blaster arena
  • Thrilling 28-foot rock climbing wall
  • Laser maze time portal
  • Interactive toddlers play section
  • Superb arcade games venue with the typhoon and virtual thrill rider roller coaster.

Feeling hungry? We have a full restaurant with a great children’s menu, adult menu, healthy Zen menu and a great variety of sweets! We have 11 private party rooms as well as a massive mezzanine that receives up to 250 people! The great view of the mezzanine looks over the entire amusement center! Book your birthday parties, sport team events, family/corporate Christmas parties, daycare and day camp outings, school outings, daycare and elementary graduations and much more! In proximity of the West Island of Montreal, Rigaud, Valleyfield, Ile-Perrot, Hudson, St-Clet, St-lazare, Laval and South Shore through highway 30. Calculate your route on Google maps! We are a short drive away! Approximately 5 minutes away from the West Island and 20 minutes from Laval!

ABOUT US
Drop In Play

Our bight, clean and open concept play space offers a large variety of toys and an array of activities to keep the busiest bodies happy and promote social, physical, and developmental play!

Birthday Parties

Your children are in for a treat when booking your parties with us! We have two party packages to choose from which include a host of benefits such as exclusive use of our venue, set up & clean up and unlimited tea, coffee and water to name but a few.

Parent Relief

Take advantage of our drop off program and give your children some play relief all while you take advantage of some uninterrupted time

After School Care

Our after school program is about providing our community with a safe, and supportive place for your children to experience new opportunities, build positive relationships and have fun!
